How to Pay and Get Your Book

  1. Select a book from the Book Exchange page and click either Exchange or Download.
  2. You’ll be redirected to this payment page. Verify the book details: Book ID and Action.
  3. Enter your Safaricom number in the phone number field and click Pay Now. The amount is automatically set to Ksh. 200.
  4. Approve the STK Push sent to your Safaricom phone to complete the payment.
  5. Once payment is successful:
    • For Exchange: Your book exchange will be confirmed, and you'll receive a notification.
    • For Download: You’ll instantly unlock the download link for the book.

Need help? Contact us at support@bookexchange.com.
